# Requirements:

|REQUIREMENTS |	Fixed |	Variable |	Wish |
|-------------|-|-|-|
|The surface of the final product must be able to be pulled by a vacuum on and picked up in line with the NEN 13155. (DUTCH standard for vacuum lifting)|x|-|-|	 
The machine must be able to process multiple different **thermoplastic** materials ranging from **180-400 degrees Celsius**.|-|x|-|
The machine will have to work with the **existing extruder**.|x|-|-| 	 
The machine will have an electrical connection to the existing extruder, to synchronize with **Emergency Stops and speed variations** during automatic mode.|x|-|-|
The semi-finished product needs to be **automatically transported to the storage**.|-|-|x|
The machine needs a user manual and cleaning instructions.|x|-|-|	 	 
The machine  has a **continuous output**.|x|-|-|
The machine must be able to process thermoplastics with glass fiber or carbon fiber with a length of **15 +- 5 mm**.|x|-|-| 	
The machine must process thermoplastic materials in such a way that at least 90% of the fibers retain their original length throughout the operation.|-|x|-|
The price is a maximum of **500 euros**.|-|-|x|
The machine makes plates with at least a thickness **consistency of max  +- 0,5 mm** |-|x|-| 	 
90% or more of the fibers need to stay at the original length.|-|x|-|	 
The machine is cleanable in at least 3 hours. |-|x|-|	 
The semi-finished sheet must be made between **2 to 4mm**. |-|x|-|	 
The machine needs to be able to produce between **1 and 10 kg / h**, and more if it is possible. |-|x|-|
